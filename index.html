About
The Sound of Miami's Nightlife

DJ OTEO is a Miami-based DJ and active producer on Fish Out Of Water Records, delivering a signature blend of Afro House, Tech House, and Afro Latin House across South Florida's most prominent nightlife venues. With multiple residencies spanning upscale supper clubs, rooftop brunches, and high-energy nightclub events, OTEO brings a consistent, premium sonic identity to every room.

Performing 4+ nights per week across diverse venues and formats, OTEO commands audiences ranging from intimate 100-person brunches to 500+ capacity club nights. His Latin-rooted approach to Afro House creates a distinctive energy that resonates with Miami's multicultural nightlife scene, making him an ideal partner for brands targeting affluent, experience-driven consumers.
